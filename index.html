Philanthropy (CSR)

Our Giving

Proverbs 11:25 : "The generous will prosper, those who refresh others will themselves be refreshed"

The Rajamani brothers, see giving to be the the cornerstone of the Kingdom philosophy, so they freely and eagerly give with open hearts, to whosoever may be in need.

Over the years this has led them to partner with many Metropolitan/ Districts/ Assemblies/ Organisations to keep up their philanthropic efforts to give. They believe that they are Stewards of God's wealth and it is only right that the share it with the less fortunate, the following are a few of their CSR efforts

  • Covid relief
     During Covid Rajamani Brothers donated Close to 1 Million GHC worth of Personal Protective Equipment, food, and sanitation supplies to the Tema East Constituency, Eastern Region, Techniman North Disctrict and the Tema Metropolitan Assembly
  • Farmer Empowerment
     On December 1st, 2022, Kingdom Exim had made a donation of GH¢500,000 to the Ministry of Food and Agriculture support the 38th National Famers Day Celebration. With an additional donation of GH¢25000 to support the Ministry in awarding the individual who would emerge as the best crop farmer for that year.
  • Medical Support
     Over the time the Rajamani Brothers Facilitated and Payed for specialized diagnostics, treatment, and surgeries for Ghanians who may have been in dire medical needs at leading hospitals in India
  • University of Ghana Support
    The Rajamani Brothers made a donation of interactive flat panel and computers to the Center of Migration Studies in order to support classroom modernisation, facilitate lectures and strenghten research and administrative work at the centre
  • Donations towards Orphanages
     over the time,the Rajamani Brothers have Donated Food items, Clothing and Monetary Funds towards countless Old age homes and Orphanages inorder to support the less fortunate.
  • Scholarship for Bright children
     Kingdom Exim have sponsored for the education of countless, needy and underprivileged children who showed good signs during primary school but may have been unable to continue their studies ,in the region where they operate, Tema
  • Water & sanitation
     The Rajamani Brothers constructed public washroom facilities at Zenu-Ashaiman in partnership with Rotary Club Ghana
  • Environmental sustainability
     in order to mitigate the challenge of climate change and also to reclaim the lost forest of the country, we provided seedlings to TMA and Ashaiman as part of the Green Ghana Initiative.By focusing on aesthetic enhancement and recreation, the greening campaign aspires not only to beautify Tema but also to promote environmental awareness among its citizens.

James Rajamani

The Trade Diplomat

James Kwame Rajamani is a Ghanaian, Governance Advisor, International Business Consultant, Philanthropist, and Business Executive whose unwavering commitment to Ghana’s socio-economic transformation defines his life’s work.

He is the co-founder and Executive Chairman of the Kingdom Exim Group. He stands at the forefront of strategic investment, trade diplomacy, and community-centered development. With over a decade of leadership experience and a reputation for results-driven governance, he plays a critical role in advancing Ghana’s economic infrastructure, job creation, and regional influence.

In 2021, he was appointed as the Chairman of the India-Africa Trade Council (IATC) for Central and West Africa. He constantly works to Improve the India-Ghana Bilateral relations in the areas of Economic Co-operation.

Being born in India, he understands the Indian terrain and culture, with which he continues to champion Ghana's interests throughout all his endeavors. He has brought several Indian and other Nation's Companies to invest in Ghana, and encourages them to deal fairly and equitably with Ghana, to assist in value creation over value extraction.

Key Focus: Infrastructure Development, International Relations, and Macro-Economic Strategy.

Immanuel Rajamani

The Executive Strategist

Immanuel Paulraj Rajamani is a Ghanaian, Business Advisor, International Business Consultant, Philanthropist, and Business Executive whose unwavering commitment to Ghana’s socio-economic transformation defines his life’s work.

He is the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of the Kingdom Exim Group. He stands at the forefront of Kingdom Exim's day to day operations and Functioning Strategy. With over a decade of capable operational experience and a reputation for results-driven governance, he plays a critical role in advancing Kingdom Exim as a pioneer in the Ghanaian Private sector

As the CEO & Co-founder of Kingdom Exim Limited, Immanuel has been instrumental in establishing a powerful presence across industries such as agriculture, shipping, hospitality, construction, mining, and more.

He holds a Doctorate in Philosophy in Executive Leadership and Business, complementing his Executive Master of Business (EMBA) in Export and Import Management. Since establishing the company in 2008, Dr. Rajamani has demonstrated strong strategic leadership and discipline, overseeing its expansion from an initial two employees to a Group that employs thousands and specializes in exporting key commodities like Raw Cashew Nuts and Soya Beans.

Key Focus: Private Sector Profitability, Shareholder Value, and Macro-Economic Strategy.
